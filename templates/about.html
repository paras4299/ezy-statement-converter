How It Works

Our advanced PDF parsing technology extracts transaction data from your bank statements with high accuracy. We support multiple bank formats including HDFC Bank, State Bank of India, and Axis Bank.

Simply upload your PDF statement, select your bank, and let our system do the rest. Within seconds, you'll have a clean, organized Excel file ready for download.

Security & Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. All uploaded files are processed securely and are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after conversion. We never store or share your financial data.

Your data is encrypted during transmission and processing, ensuring complete security throughout the conversion process.
